ABOUT SNHS

Science National Honor Society is a club open to all high schoolers who have taken an AP science class and have a cumulative GPA of at least 3.50 in all science classes. The club is focused on promoting participation in science, research opportunities, and service opportunities for members. Service activities include trail clean ups, tutoring, and other events.

We meet once a month during advisory in the lecture hall.

In order to qualify for a cord at graduation, SNHS members must have at least 10 hours of service or research related hours by their senior year.

To maintain yearly membership, students must complete 5 hours of service by the end of the year.
